Queen Elizabeth II

Here is the thank you letter written on my name from The Queen Elizabeth II. This story begins with my insatiable desire to send my handmade gift to Queen Elizabeth II. I started practicing to make portrait dolls of marilyn monroe and Frida Kahlo. when I saw that i have mastered the technique the third portrait doll was Queen Elizabeth's.

We chose her one of the most famous looks as the design of the doll. We used the following materials: Pearls, Crystals, Aquamarine.

I could have never dreamed of receiving the "Thank you letter" from Queen Elizabeth II.
