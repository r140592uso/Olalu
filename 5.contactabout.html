About Us

Handcraft was my childhood passion, but no one took it seriously, including me. Before starting O'lalu I worked in the media then I was a military. Because of my family circumstances I had to give up my military contract and I became a housewife and a full time mother. I then realised I wanted to create something for myself, I was missing handcrafting. I started embroidery, knitting, beading and I learned many different techniques. Handcrafting became a sort of therapy for me.

In 2018 I realised it was time for me to start a new venture and I opened a page on one of the social networks and started selling my creations. I was still in search of the right product. I switched to creating jewellery with natural minerals and then dolls. Dolls became very popar and the number of orders increased. In the beginning my pieces were simple but with a lot of hard work, dedication and overtime, my dolls become a lot more elaborated.I believe now is the time to make my creations known globally.
